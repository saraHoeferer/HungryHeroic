What's the cause ?

How bad is food waste in Austria

Food waste in Austria is a significant concern, with private households playing a substantial role in its magnitude. It is estimated that around 42% of the total food waste in the country originates from homes, amounting to approximately 577,000 tons of discarded food annually. The consequences of food waste are far-reaching.

Environmentally, it leads to unnecessary greenhouse gas emissions and the wastage of precious resources such as water and land.

Socially, food waste represents missed opportunities to address hunger and food insecurity within Austria.

By taking small but impactful steps, such as planning meals in advance, buying only what is needed, and finding creative ways to use leftovers, individuals can contribute to minimizing food waste in Austria.

Features

What is HungryHeroic good for?

Track your Groceries & Storage

Always know what kind of groceries you already have at home and what you still need to get with the next shopping trip. Whit the help of the progress bar it is easy to check when your groceries will expire.
Simply add, change and delete the Items in your personal shopping and inventory lists to always keep trace of your groceries.

Warning before food expires

In your Account you will always see the expiration date of the Items in your inventory list. And the color of the progress bar will warn you before the groceries go bad. In the Mobile Version of our Project you will be notified before a items exceeds it's expiration date. Also when you add a new item to your inventory list the software will automatically suggest an expiration date bases on the food group of the item.

Recipe Ideas based on stored food

Also an important feature of the Project is to automatically search and suggest delicious Recipes with ingredients of you inventory list that are about to expire soon. This will help you to be aware of your own food waste and to minimize it.
